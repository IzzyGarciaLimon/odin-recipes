Reuben Sandwhich

A reuben sandwhich is a grilled sandwhich on rye bread with corned beef meat. It contains swiss cheese and sauerkraut alongside the corned beef usually drenched in Russian or Thousand Island dressing.

Ingredients

  • Rye
  • Corned Beef
  • sauerkraut
  • Swiss Chesse
  • Dressing of choice

Steps

  1. Spread dressing on one side of each piece of bread
  2. Assemble the sandwhich and butter each side of the sandwhich
  3. Butter side down place the sandwhich on a hot griddle and flip once toasted.
  4. Repeat for other side
